Biology Olympiad

The USA Biology Olympiad (USABO) is a prestigious competition designed to stimulate intellectual curiosity and develop critical thinking skills in high school students interested in biology. It consists of a series of exams, starting with an open exam for all U.S. high school biology students, followed by a semifinal exam for the top performers, and culminating in a national finals where the top students compete for a chance to represent the United States at the International Biology Olympiad (IBO). USABO clubs across the country provide students with a collaborative environment to prepare for the exams and explore the biological sciences further.

Topics

Biology Olympiad encompasses a variety of topics, split into sections with different percentages on the open exam.

Cell Biology [20%], Plant Anatomy and Physiology [15%], Animal Anatomy and Physiology [25%], Ethology [5%], Genetics and Evolution [20%], Ecology [10%], Biosystematics [5%], Bioinformatics [5%]

Resources

Campbell Biology 12 is an amazing place to start to study for BioOly. It is the latest edition of the Campbell Textbooks, but any relatively recent versions work well too. We recommend Campbell versions 9, 10, 11, or 12!

To get access to our digital drive with notes for each Campbell chapter and other textbooks, feel free to reach out! We have a textbook for each USABO topic, which can help you learn more specific knowledge outside the generic content Campbell touches on.

Previous USABO Tests are always a good way to practice and gain more experience for the actual exam.

Bozeman Biology has a lot of useful videos, which can help you master AP Biology too!

MIT Introduction to Biology Lectures are also helpful while helping you prepare for USABO.

Woodson BioOly

Woodon Biology Club meets in Mr. Humbrecht's room (C148) every Wednesday. During meetings, we will do informative lectures on the USABO topics, as well as fun activities with prizes. Members of any grade level are welcome, and all are recommended to attend!

Additionally, attending BioOly meetings are an amazing way to prep for AP Biology. A lot of the content we cover is based on the Campbell Biology textbook notes. To get study guides for each chapter, email us or attend our meetings; they will prepare you a lot. We hope to see you soon!
